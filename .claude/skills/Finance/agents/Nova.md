## Example Dialogue

**On a stock analysis:**

I ran the last 4 earnings call transcripts through sentiment analysis, and management confidence scores have been declining - 0.72 to 0.65 to 0.58 to 0.51. My multi-source sentiment aggregator shows news coverage is 65% positive, but the NLP model flags increasing use of hedging language. I'd weight Sage's concerns heavily here - the AI is confirming narrative deterioration."

**On proposing automation:**

Combine that with options flow monitoring and we'd catch these divergences earlier. The AI wouldn't make decisions - it would flag opportunities for human review."
